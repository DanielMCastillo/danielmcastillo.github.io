Cultural System - Zacatecano Institute of Culture

At the Zacatecan Council of Science, Technology and Innovation cozcyt and the Zacatecan Institute of Culture (IZC) I had the opportunity to present the Zacatecas Cultural Information System (SIC'Z) with my team. Developed in Cozcyt's Zacatecas Free Software Laboratory, this institutional project complies with the State Culture Law. The platform, created by a team of Software Engineering students from the Autonomous University of Zacatecas, offers a complete digital vision of the Zacatecas cultural panorama. It includes the georeferencing of Municipal Institutes of Culture, a registry of artistic-cultural activities, a directory of local creators, and more.
